Biography

Hello, my name is Bryon Beaufait. I am 33 years old living in New Jersey. I am currently a student at Raritan valley Community College studying interface design & web development. I have been a chef/cook for the past 10 years and decided to make a transition to a different career path. Every day is a learning experience in which I am excited to learn and grow my knowledge and understandings.

In my free time I enjoy cooking, playing video games, enjoying the outdoors music and Television. I have learned that web design is a lot like cooking in many ways. The more practice you have and time working on the craft the better you get. I still have much to learn but I am excited to see where this new journey takes me.

Hovers

Hover effects occur when a user hovers over an element with their mouse and an effect occurs. This effect can consist of color change, fades in or out, growing, movement such as bouncing and many more. Hover effects bring a different style to a website in new and exciting ways.
